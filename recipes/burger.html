Burger Recipe

Description

Here we have a world famous delicious burger recipe that'll make any cyborg scream. This burger has halal beef that should be generously seasoned, and is topped to perfection.

Ingredients

  • halal Beef
  • Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Pickles
  • onion
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Condiments (mayo,ketchup,mustard)

Steps

  1. Generously season your halal beef and cook it well done.
  2. Toast the inside of your buns before adding mayo and lettuce, then your patty to the bottom bun.
  3. Add cheese if you'd like.
  4. Add your pickles, tomato, and onions
  5. Top will ketchup, mustard, and the top bun
  6. Finish the recipe by microwaving the entire burger.
  7. Enjoy!
